Become a Personal Shopper in 4 Easy Steps

Step 1

Complete your HelpMate Application

Apply to HelpMate and complete the KYC verification. When you pass the verification, you will be able to schedule an appointment to complete your in-person onboarding.

Step 2

Get access to local users that need your help/assistance

After onboarding session, you're ready to start picking up orders. Login to your account to view available orders in your area of interest. Once you're confirmed,it's yours! You can accept as many orders based on your availability.

Step 3

Get paid weekly and enjoy work benefits

Login on the site when you're ready to start working. Logout once you're ready to stop working. You will be asked to rate your experience with the paired employers. The employers will also give you a rating out of 5 stars, based on your performance. You will get paid directly to your bank account each Friday for the prior week.

Step 4

Build your reputation through your profile and access higher-paying orders

Collect good reviews from employers and build your reputation. As your profile improves,you will be more likely to be selected for higher-paying jobs.
